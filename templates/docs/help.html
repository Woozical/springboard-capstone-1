How-To LinkBin:

Links

Add link URLs to the text area in the control panel, then click the large link button to add them to your repo. You can input multiple URLs at once, just separate them on a new line before clicking the link button. When submitting your links, they will be instantly added to your repository list on the left. We will try to grab some metadata about your URLs and fill out your entries accordingly, but this may take a few seconds.

Make sure your links include the HTTP schema! (http:// or https://)

Text Boxes and Dividers

To the left and right of the link button are two buttons to add text boxes and dividers. Dividers are useful for separating your links and text boxes into different categories (e.g. Social Media, Downloads). Text boxes are useful for providing instructions or for when a link or category needs a more verbose description. A text box may also include an image, just provide a link to the image in the Image field when editing the entry.

Both text boxes and dividers can be used to link to URLs.

Repo Controls

Changes to your repository are not automatically saved to the server. Whenever you have unsaved changes on your repository, the save button will be yellow. If you make any mistakes, remember that you can always refresh the page without saving your changes first. Next to the save button are buttons to edit your repository's information (i.e. title, description, privacy mode), or delete your repository entirely, and return to the home page.

Always check to see if you have unsaved changes before navigating away!

Entry Controls

Your entries can be re-sorted using the up and down carets to the left of each entry. Each entry's information can be edited by clicking on the gear icon to bring up the editing window. Here you can manually set an entry's image preview, or give it a rating to denote its importance.

Entries can be quickly deleted by holding CTRL while clicking on the gear icon.
